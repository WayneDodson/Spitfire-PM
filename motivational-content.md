# Motivational Quotes & Messages System

## Psychological Principles Applied

### 1. **Future Self Visualization**
Help users imagine their future success to increase motivation and commitment.

### 2. **Progress Acknowledgment**
Celebrate every win, no matter how small, to build confidence and momentum.

### 3. **Growth Mindset Language**
Use language that emphasizes learning and improvement over fixed ability.

### 4. **Social Proof & Belonging**
Show users they're part of a community of learners and future PMs.

### 5. **Autonomy & Competence**
Reinforce that users are in control and building real, valuable skills.

## Implementation Notes

- **Personalization**: Use user's name when available
