# Project Pro: 7-Level PM Certification Program

## Course Overview
A comprehensive 42-hour project management certification program designed to take learners from complete beginners to job-ready project managers capable of commanding competitive salaries. Structured to mirror industry-standard PM certifications while providing hands-on, practical experience.

---

## Level 1: PM Foundations (Free)
**Title:** "Introduction to Project Management"  
**Subtitle:** "Build Your PM Foundation"  
**Estimated Time:** 6 hours  
**Access:** Free for all users

### Description
Start your project management journey by understanding what PMs actually do. Learn the core concepts, methodologies, and skills that form the foundation of successful project management. Through three realistic scenarios across healthcare, construction, and technology sectors, you'll make real decisions and see their consequences.

### Learning Outcomes
- Understand the role and responsibilities of a project manager
- Differentiate between Waterfall, Agile, and Scrum methodologies
- Identify project stakeholders and their needs
- Learn basic project planning and timeline creation
- Practice decision-making in real-world scenarios

### Content Modules
1. **What is Project Management?** (45 min)
   - The PM role in modern organizations
   - Key responsibilities and daily activities
   - Career paths and salary expectations

2. **PM Methodologies Overview** (1 hour)
   - Waterfall: Sequential project management
   - Agile: Iterative and flexible approach
   - Scrum: Sprint-based team collaboration
   - When to use each methodology

3. **Interactive Scenarios** (4 hours)
   - Healthcare: Commission a new hospital ward
   - Construction: Renovate a Victorian home
   - Technology: Corporate website redesign

4. **Level 1 Assessment** (15 min)
   - 20-question quiz covering all Level 1 content
   - Must score 70% to proceed to Level 2

**Subtitle:** "From Concept to Kickoff"  
**Estimated Time:** 6 hours  
**Access:** Unlock by referring 1 friend

   - Buffer management and contingency planning

3. **Risk Management Fundamentals** (1.5 hours)
   - Risk identification techniques
   - Qualitative and quantitative risk assessment
   - Risk mitigation strategies
   - Creating risk registers

4. **Interactive Scenarios** (45 min)
   - Manufacturing: Launch a new product line
   - Retail: Open a new store location
   - Software: Develop a mobile app

5. **Level 2 Assessment** (15 min)
   - 25-question quiz covering Levels 1-2

## Salary Expectations After Completion

### Entry-Level PM (0-2 years experience)
- **UK:** £30,000 - £45,000
- **US:** $60,000 - $80,000
- **Roles:** Junior PM, Project Coordinator, Assistant PM

### Mid-Level PM (2-5 years experience)
- **UK:** £45,000 - £70,000
- **US:** $80,000 - $120,000
- **Roles:** Project Manager, Scrum Master, Program Coordinator

### Senior PM (5+ years experience)
- **UK:** £70,000 - £100,000+
- **US:** $120,000 - $180,000+
- **Roles:** Senior PM, Program Manager, PMO Director
